Give feedback to schools

Use this service to:

  • tell Ofsted what you think about your child’s school

By giving feedback you are helping schools to raise their standards plus parents and carers to make more informed choices about where to send their children.

All feedback is anonymised and will be read by an Ofsted inspector to help them build a picture of the school.

Ofsted also uses this information to make decisions about which schools to inspect and when.

By giving feedback you consent to your responses being made available to Ofsted.

This service is just for feedback about schools. There is a different service to make a complaint about your school or get in touch with the school directly if you have other concerns.
