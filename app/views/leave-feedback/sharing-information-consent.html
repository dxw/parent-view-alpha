What happens with your feedback

All feedback is anonymised and will be read by an Ofsted inspector to help them understand what {{ schoolName }} is like. They may also share feedback with the school. Ofsted also uses this information to make decisions about which schools to inspect and when.

By giving feedback you give your consent to Ofsted and the school seeing your responses.
